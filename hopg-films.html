HOPG Films

Graphite optics are highly stable. Due to the increased thermal conductivity, high radiation and thermal stability.

Thin films of HOPG are used as stripping material in cyclotrons. The films strip electrons from the accelerated particles and are subjected to extreme thermal loads. The structural features of the HOPG-flex form permit to produce films with a size of a few cm2 and only of 1 to 5 µm thick. The high purity, high electrical and thermal conductivity, as well as minimum stress within the film ensure a long service life under experimental condition and make those films very popular among the cyclotron community [Rao, Y.-N , 2013], [Bylinskii, Yu,2018].
